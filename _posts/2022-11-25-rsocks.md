# RSOCKS

**RSOCKS** is a russian botnet proxy service that was operated by Russians, it expanded from targetting IoT to Andriods and conventional computers. 
RSOCKS  offers the ip addresses of a compromised device, The cost for access to a pool of RSOCKS proxies ranged from $30 per day for access to 2,000 proxies to $200 per day for access to 90,000 proxies.

RSOCKS compromised devices by *brute-forcing* their credentials and installing softwares on the breached computer to turn them into *proxy servers*.  

> **On June 2022 RSOCKS was shutfown by the US Department of Justice.**
